Design Principles

Kevin Castro

Alignment

Tesla

The Tesla's site has the logo and the menu icon perfectly aligned with the buttons, and clearly creates this invisible lines along the borders. Also, the Model, text, image, buttons and icon on the bottom are center aligned

Repetition

Amazon

We can see many times the characteristic color of Amazon in the logo, the cart icon and the search button. That creates a good pattern making us remember the brand. The white color is used in several elements as well and there's a slightly different version of the logo in the banner

Contrast

Apple

In Apple's website, the dark background allows to completely focus on the content in the hero. The white text is very readable, the call to action links stand out with that vibrant blue color, and they made impossible to not look at the iPad image with those colors
