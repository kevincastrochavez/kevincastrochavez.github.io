Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide an amazing user experience when consulting the weather and provide useful information as the same time, like places of interest and funny things to do based on the weather from three different locations. This site will be responsive and designed with a mobile-first approach to allow a good distribution of the site and its content on all kinds of devices (cell phones, laptops, tablets).
